More About the Orgs:

  • 1. Marietta Gays Queer Liberation Front
    • Connected Center: Unity, LGBTQ+

    We provide an online community with frequent in person events on the Marietta campus. We also organize and advocate the best we can for queer rights in our community. (The website you're on right now is up-kept by this group.)

  • 2. Kennesaw Pride Alliance
    • Connected Center: LGBTQ+

    According to our mission statement, our organization promotes awareness, understanding, and acceptance of Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Intersex, Transgender, Queer, Asexual, Aromantic, and/or questioning individuals and their issues through activism, advocacy, social activities, and community service. The Kennesaw Pride Alliance seeks to provide a support network for the students of Kennesaw State University who identify as LGBTQ+, allies, as well as educate others to challenge stereotypes associated with LGBTQ+ individuals and communities.

  • 3. House of Owllustrators
    • Connected Center: LGBTQ+

    organization for KSU students who animate

  • 4. oSTEM @ KSU
    • Connected Center: LGBTQ+

    We host events to serve as safe spaces for people in STEM who identify as part of the LGBTQ+ community.

  • 5. The Church Lot
    • Connected Centers: LGBTQ+, Women, Unity

    Holding meeting space and providing spiritual support.

  • 6. K-Pop Club
    • Connected Centers: LGBTQ+, Cultural Awareness, Global Village

    The K-pop club offers a safe and inclusive space for those interested, passionate, and culturally aligned with Korean pop culture. We encourage an atmosphere of acceptance with zero tolerance for bigotry.

  • 7. Young Democratic Socialists of America
    • Connected Centers: General Safe Space

    KSU chapter of the largest socialist organization in the country.

  • 8. EcoOwls: Students for Sustainability
    • Connected Centers: Global Village, Unity

    Providing a down-to-earth space for collaboration, mutual understanding, and support for KSU students. Many members are passionate about sustainability and volunteerism, creating a welcoming environment.

  • 9. KSU Women in Animation Collective
    • Connected Centers: LGBTQ+, Women, Cultural Awareness

    We host speaker events featuring women, queer folks, and people of color, plus workshops on animation history and marginalized identities.

  • 10. The Creative Club
    • Connected Center: LGBTQ+

    We’re providing a safe space for queer folks at and near KSU to express themselves creatively.

  • 11. United Campus Workers of Georgia at KSU
    • Connected Centers: General Safe Space

    Organized around protection of DEI programs and people affected by unjust ICE enforcement.

  • 12. Hello Kitty and Friends Club
    • Connected Centers: LGBTQ+, Women

    We provide a safe space for affected groups to make friends, relax in a supportive environment, and hang out! Women- and LGBTQ+-run.

  • 13. Classical Latin Club
    • Connected Centers: LGBTQ+, Women

    Although we can’t replace resource centers, we show support and acknowledge our meetings may serve as a safe space for students.

  • 14. Student Organization of Politics
    • Connected Centers: LGBTQ+, Women, Cultural Awareness, Global Village, Unity, HOLA Services

    Offering an outlet for discussion and avenues to mobilize and become politically active.

  • 15. Model United Nations
    • Connected Centers: Cultural Awareness, Global Village

    A supportive space for students to dive into global issues, debate real-world policies, and build skills in diplomacy and teamwork.

  • 16. OwlRadio
    • Connected Centers: General Safe Space

    Owl Radio is Kennesaw State University's official student-run streaming radio station.

  • B. psychological Services
    • Connected Centers: General Safe Space

    Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) helps students develop the skills necessary to better manage their emotions, navigate relationships, and address other mental wellness concerns. These free to students services will help you get through these tough times.
