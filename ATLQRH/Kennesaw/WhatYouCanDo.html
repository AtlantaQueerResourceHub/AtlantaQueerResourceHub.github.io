What you can do

In this day and age, visibility is important. Be visibly you (within reasonable safety). Read up on the gay liberation movements of the past and get inspired by the stories. Don't take the cisgender heterosexual world we live in to be the be all end all, if theres something about it you don't like, carve an avenue for yourself and others to not have to deal with it. Liberation is about freeing ourselves from the expectations set upon us by society.

YDSA (Young Democratic Socialists of America

This organization is a subchapter of Atlanta DSA located on the KSU Kennesaw Campus. DSA is focussed on worker's rights through unionization and electoralism. KSU's DSA chapter is a registered student organization and have an official owl life page. Please reach out on OwlLife to learn more or get involved.

Atlanta DSA (Democratic Socialists of America)

Atlanta DSA is the Atlanta chapter of the Demecratic Socialists of America. DSA is focussed on worker's rights through unionization and electoralism.Their efforts have paid off with a DSA member being elected to the Georgia State house of representatives in 2024. DSA is a strong grassroots movement that moves fast and is always needing more volenteers. I cannot stress ho easy it is to get involved, just show up to any meeting (locations and dates on their instagram) and they will help you get started

Bread and Roses Front

Bread and roses front is a more decentralized approach to political activism focussed on projects and mutual aid. This group is quite small and new but has great ambitions. If you have any social projects you'd like help on or would like to help with existing projects such as this one, please reach out on instagram.
