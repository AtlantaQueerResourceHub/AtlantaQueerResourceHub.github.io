Queer Friendly Clubs

  • KSU pride alliance

    • KSU pride alliance is a predominantly K-Campus based organization who describes themselves as providing a support network for LGBTQ+ students of Kennesaw State University and to educate others surrounding the realities of LGBTQ+ experiences.

  • LGBTQ+ Resource Center RSO/Discord Server

    • Link to the Discord Server trying to serve as the successor to the LGBTQ+ Center. They are also trying to become an RSO to restore some of the resources lost.

  • KSU Creative Club

    • KSU Creative Club is a space for creative individuals and people who like to make fun projects with friends

  • Board Game Club

    • KSU Board Game Club is queer run organization that meets every friday at 5 on Marietta campus. Good place to make friends.

  • oSTEM

    • oSTEM is a professional organization aimed at celebrating LGBTQ+ individuals in STEM

Queer Friendly Groups

  • Queer Workout Groups

    • Ever wanted to get into working out but gender norms kept you from it? This group can help, it's primarily Kennesaw based but Marietta students are in it as well.
